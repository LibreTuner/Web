Why use LibreTuner?

Fast

LibreTuner is written in Rust to achieve maximum performance while ensuring safety, keeping your ECU safe from random crashes while reflashing.

Cross Platform

LibreTuner is designed to work on most popular platforms including Windows, Linux, macOS, and soon, Android. Not every platform supports the same capabilities and flashing is limited to Windows and Linux.

Extensible

Definition files use a simple YAML format that's easy to use so you don't have to read large manuals.

Open Source

LibreTuner is built from the contributions of developers who care about providing enthusiasts with a reliable method of tuning.

A suite of tools

LibreTuner provides an entire suite of tools needed to tune and diagnose your car including a ROM downloader, tune editor, ROM flashing utility, diagnostic trouble code scanner, and datalogging utilities.
